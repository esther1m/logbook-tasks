Since a young age, Esther has loved to read, often checking out 6 books from the library to read within the three week loaning period. As Esther grew, she lost interest in reading as it was now a required activity for school as opposed to something she does in her spare time for fun. Then came the rise of BOOKTOK with all it's creative approaches to immersive yourself in reading. Annotating, tabbing and talking about your favourite books with enthusiasm - reading had become trendy.
